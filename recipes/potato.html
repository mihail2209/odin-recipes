Potato

Description:

Baked Potato Wedges with so many amazing flavours. Just as popular as fries, wedges are the perfect appetizer or snack!

Ingredients:

  • potato
  • sunflower oil
  • garlic
  • salt

Steps:

  1. Cut the potatoes into slices
  2. put sunflower oil on the slices
  3. salt the potatoes
  4. grate garlic on a fine grater and apply to the potatoes
  5. bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes at a temperature of 200 degrees
