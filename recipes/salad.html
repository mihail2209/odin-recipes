Salad

Description:

Tomato salad with cucumber and onion will refresh any table. It can be prepared for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Ingredients:

  • tomato
  • cucumber
  • onion
  • garlic
  • sunflower oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps:

  1. chop tomatoes and cucumbers
  2. chop onion and garlic
  3. add sunflower oil
  4. add salt and pepper
