Mushroom sauce

Liquid seasoning for dishes, sauce, the main ingredient of which are mushrooms - both fresh and dried, salted and pickled.

Mushroom sauces are widely used in cooking due to the significant amount of flavoring and aromatic substances contained in mushrooms.

  • onion
  • garlic
  • sunflower oil
  • mushrooms
  • sour cream
  • dill
  1. chop onion
  2. chop garlic
  3. fry in a frying pan with sunflower oil
  4. chop mushrooms and add to the pan
  5. add sour cream
  6. add chopped dill
